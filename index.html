How to Play zkMonopoly

Welcome to zkMonopoly, a solo blockchain game on the Horizen Gobi Testnet! Follow these steps to play and earn XMONO points using zero-knowledge proofs.

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Click "CONNECT WALLET" to link your MetaMask wallet to the Ethereum Sepolia Testnet. Ensure you have ETH for gas fees.
  2. Start the Game: Click "üöÄ START GAME" to receive 500 SMONO tokens and begin your journey.
  3. Roll the Dice: Click "üé≤ ROLL DICE" to move around the board. Landing on tiles can earn (PROFIT) or deduct (LOSS) SMONO tokens.
  4. Earn SMONO Tokens: Collect at least 1000 SMONO tokens by landing on PROFIT tiles.
  5. Claim XMONO Points: When you have 1000 SMONO, click "üî• ZKVERIFY/Claim XMONO" to generate a zero-knowledge proof, verify it on-chain, burn 1000 SMONO, and earn 1 XMONO point! Optionally, enter a zkVerify API key (from Horizen Labs) for additional off-chain verification.
  6. Check Your Progress: View your ETH balance, SMONO tokens, XMONO points, and game log in the UI panel. Click tiles to see their details.
